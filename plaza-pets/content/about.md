# About Plaza Pets

## Our Mission
To provide the Fordsburg community with high-quality pet products and foster a culture of responsible and loving pet ownership.

## Our Story
Located in the heart of Oriental Plaza, Plaza Pets was founded with a simple goal: to make pet care accessible and enjoyable for everyone in Johannesburg. We believe every pet deserves the best.

## Why Choose Us?
We are more than just a store; we are pet lovers serving pet lovers. We understand the unique needs of our local community.
